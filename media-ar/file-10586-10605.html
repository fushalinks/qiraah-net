About the circumstances of [his] arriving at this invention al-Abyad said in an exclusive statement to Asharq Alawsat that the idea had come to fruition [lit. fermented/ripened] since around four years [ago] and he registered the patent of the invention on 24 November (Tishreen al-Thani) 2008.
